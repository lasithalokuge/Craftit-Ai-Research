## Video 1: "How I Create $1k/Day Dropshipping Video Ads using AI"
**Creator:** The Ecom King | **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lnjPuG0DSyI

### Workflow (Step-by-Step)
1. **Product research** — Understand the product deeply (example: red light face mask)
2. **Competitor intelligence** — Google search → find competitors → check Facebook Ad Library for active ads
3. **Identify winning ads** — Look for same video running multiple times (signals profitability)
4. **Download competitor's winning ad** — Chrome extension "Ad Library" for free download
5. **Script generation** — Paste custom prompt into ChatGPT "TikTok Viral Script Writer" GPT + upload competitor video + competitor ad library link
6. **Generate 5 scripts** across different angles (anti-aging, redness, spa-at-home, tech/science, etc.)
7. **Format scripts** — Request 30-45 seconds, no table format, split each script into 3 parts for 3 different avatars
8. **Create AI avatars (Premium)** — Arcads: paste script segment, select HD female avatar, generate (~5 min each)
9. **Create AI avatars (Free)** — CapCut Commerce Pro: avatars section, paste script, turn off captions
10. **Generate B-roll/hooks** — CapCut Commerce Pro "Video Generator": paste Shopify URL → generates 10-30 AI product videos
11. **Source additional B-roll** — Download TikTok videos of product in use
12. **Face swap** — AI headshot generator + aifaceswap.io to replace faces in downloaded TikTok content
13. **Edit in CapCut** — 9:16 format, layer avatars chronologically, add B-roll overlays, auto-caption with templates, add background music, add CTA with website preview

### Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Google / Facebook Ad Library | Competitor research & winning ad identification |
| Ad Library Chrome Extension | Download competitor video ads |
| SimilarWeb Chrome Extension | Estimate competitor revenue |
| ChatGPT (TikTok Viral Script Writer GPT) | Script generation from competitor data |
| **Arcads** (paid, recommended) | Premium HD AI avatars |
| **CapCut Commerce Pro** (free) | Free AI avatars + AI Video Generator for B-roll/hooks |
| TikTok | Source B-roll footage |
| AI headshot generator (unnamed) | Create fake headshots for face swap |
| **aifaceswap.io** | Video face swap to anonymize downloaded content |
| **CapCut** (editor) | Final video editing, captions, music |

### Inputs Needed
- Product name, description, 3 key features
- Competitor's winning video ad (downloaded)
- Competitor's Facebook Ad Library URL
- Shopify store URL (for CapCut Commerce Pro)
- B-roll footage from TikTok

### Video Structure Pattern
- **Hook** (3-5s): AI-generated montage from CapCut Commerce Pro (e.g., "Age beautifully" with quick transitions)
- **Problem** (5-10s): Avatar 1 describes the pain point, B-roll of skin issues overlaid
- **Solution** (10-15s): Avatar 2 introduces product features, B-roll of product in action
- **CTA** (5s): Avatar 3 with "Click shop below" + website checkout preview
- **Total:** 30-45 seconds, 9:16 vertical, 3 different AI avatars per video
- Background music throughout (quiet, removes "dull AI effect")

### Key Insights
- **Split scripts across 3 avatars** — performs better than single avatar
- **Multiple angles per product** — generate 5+ scripts with different hooks/angles
- **Winning ad intelligence** — base scripts on competitor ads that are running multiple times (proven winners)
- **B-roll is critical** — never just show AI avatar talking; always overlay relevant footage
- **Face swap for compliance** — replace real people's faces in downloaded content
- **10-30 min per video** is realistic editing time

## Video 2: "This AI TikTok Shop Video Made Me $67,420"
**Creator:** Harry Chang (interviewed) | **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Iv8DddAIXLw

### Workflow (Step-by-Step)
1. **Find product on TikTok Shop** — Use Creator's Corner for early access to emerging products
2. **Deep research on ChatGPT** — Find unique product angles (e.g., saffron → crocetin antioxidant → liver health)
3. **Study market on TikTok** — Watch 5-10 videos of similar products to understand what works
4. **Build swipe file** — Save every good AI and non-AI marketing video in Notion
5. **Get transcripts of viral videos** — Use "Script Talk Audit" transcript downloader on high-view-count videos (e.g., Dr. Berg 15M views)
6. **Adapt script** — Cut out half of transcript, replace with product introduction; ChatGPT does 90% of writing
7. **Script framework:** Interesting topic → Problem → Introduce solution → Plug product → CTA
8. **Create "reaction person"** — Use **Veo 3** (Google) to generate a person in a car with bright shirt reacting/introducing
9. **Create "stage speaker"** — Generate image with **Krea AI** (African-American woman on stage with mic), then animate with **HeyGen** using **ElevenLabs** voice
10. **Edit in CapCut** — Remove background from reaction person, overlay in corner, freeze frame, add captions with templates

### Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| TikTok Shop / Creator's Corner | Product discovery |
| ChatGPT (Deep Research) | Product research, angles, script writing (90% of work) |
| Script Talk Audit | Transcript extraction from viral videos |
| Notion | Swipe file / script storage |
| **Veo 3** (Google) | Generate reaction/intro person video from prompt |
| **Krea AI** | Generate AI image of stage speaker |
| **HeyGen** | Animate image into talking video |
| **ElevenLabs** | Voice generation for stage speaker |
| **CapCut** | Video editing, background removal, captions |

### Inputs Needed
- Product from TikTok Shop
- ChatGPT research on product benefits/angles
- Transcript from viral video in same niche (as script base)
- Prompts for Veo 3, Krea, HeyGen

### Video Structure Pattern (The "$67K Format")
- **Reaction hook** (3-5s): Person in car with bright/yellow shirt, reacting dramatically ("Did y'all see what she just said?!")
- **Freeze frame** of stage speaker (builds anticipation)
- **Stage speaker delivers content** (60-90s): Educational-style, discussing health/product topic in depth
- **Product plug** comes late (1:15+ into a ~3 min video)
- **Total:** ~3 minutes long (much longer than typical)
- Format: Two-layer composition (reaction person in corner + main speaker)

### Key Insights
- **Long-form converts on TikTok organic** — 3-minute videos, product mention delayed until 1:15+
- **"Nigerian secret" / cultural intrigue angles** work extremely well for health products
- **Test scripts rapidly** — this was the 7th or 8th script tested; write new scripts daily
- **Post same script 20 times** — don't just post once
- **Color theory matters** — yellow shirt on reaction person for attention
- **Compliance is key** — don't make medical claims; keep it simple and factual to avoid bans
- **Affiliate model** — 30-35% commission on TikTok Shop (up to 50-60% with special access)
- **Hit multiple trigger points** — one video must hit many psychological triggers to convert
- **Topic > Hook** — the overall topic/message matters more than just the hook

## Video 3: "I Recreated a Viral TikTok Ad with AI — Topview"
**Creator:** (unnamed reviewer) | **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lOjMWjDG2cY

### Workflow (Step-by-Step)
1. **Find viral reference video** on TikTok (e.g., shampoo recommendation video)
2. **Download reference video** — Right-click download on TikTok
3. **Upload to TopView** — Provide reference video + product image
4. **Configure settings** — 9:16 format, answer product questions (selling points, target users, target countries, language)
5. **TopView auto-generates** — Analyzes reference video (positioning, script, what was said), creates storyboard, generates new video with AI avatar + product
6. **Alternative: Product Avatar mode** — Select avatar from library, upload product image, auto/manual product placement, paste custom script, select AI voice, add subtitles
7. **Export and post**

### Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| TikTok / TikTok Studio | Find trending/viral reference videos |
| **TopView** | All-in-one: analyzes reference video, generates script, creates AI avatar video with product |

### Inputs Needed
- Reference video URL or download
- Product image
- Product selling points (natural ingredients, hair thickening, etc.)
- Target user description
- Target countries
- Language preference

### Video Structure Pattern
- **Short format:** ~15 seconds
- Host intro → Product demo → Feature highlight → Brand close-up → Selling point
- Mimics UGC style of reference video
- Subtitles auto-added (various style templates)

### Key Insights
- **Lowest effort of all tools** — paste URL + product image, answer a few questions, done
- **Reference video cloning** — TopView specifically designed to recreate viral video formats with your product
- **Multi-language support** — can generate in English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, etc.
- **Split testing built in** — "Change scripts" button lets you create 10 versions with different scripts/avatars
- **Product priority vs Model priority** — choose whether product appearance or avatar appearance is more important
- **Subtitle placement issue** — auto subtitles may overlap product; may need to add in TikTok instead

## Video 4: "Using AI to Make TikTok Shop Affiliate Product Videos (Creatify)"
**Creator:** (unnamed) | **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=S-990Zaa0TA

### Workflow (Step-by-Step)
1. **Find proven product** — Use Kalodata or browse TikTok Shop manually for hot-selling items
2. **Find high-quality Amazon listing** — Need one with lots of reviews, videos, photos, detailed description
3. **Paste Amazon URL into Creatify** — Auto-analyzes listing, pulls all assets (title, description, photos, videos)
4. **Configure** — 9:16 aspect ratio, video length (15s recommended), optional target audience
5. **AI generates 3 scripts** — Can generate more, or paste your own (DIY mode)
6. **Select/edit script** — Remove brand names for reusability, adjust messaging
7. **Edit video in Creatify** — Remove music (add in TikTok instead), adjust subtitle style, rearrange asset clips, add CTA with arrow pointing to shop link
8. **Render and export** (~$5-6 per video)

### Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Kalodata** | Product research — shows hottest TikTok Shop products by category |
| TikTok Shop | Manual product browsing |
| Amazon | Source high-quality product listings with assets |
| **Creatify.ai** | All-in-one: URL analysis → script generation → video creation → editing → CTA |

### Inputs Needed
- Amazon product URL (with videos, photos, descriptions)
- Optional: target audience description
- Optional: custom script

### Video Structure Pattern
- **15 seconds optimal** (easiest to edit, good conversion)
- Problem statement → Product as solution → Key features → CTA with arrow to shop link
- Product-focused (no avatar talking head by default — pure product footage)
- "On sale now" CTA overlay with arrow pointing to TikTok Shop link position

### Key Insights
- **15-second videos perform best** for TikTok Shop affiliates (surprising)
- **Amazon listing as input source** — brilliant hack; high-quality listings have pre-made product videos
- **Remove brand names** from scripts so video can be reused across different product links
- **Remove music before export** — add trending TikTok sounds instead to avoid muting
- **Problem-solution script framework** preferred ("Struggling with X? Try these…")
- **~$40/month, ~$5-6/video** — very cost-effective
- **Green screen AI avatar option** exists but product-only video preferred
- **CTA arrow pointing to shop link** is a specific conversion tactic

## Video 5: "How to Make Ultra Realistic VIRAL AI Ads for Any Brand in 2026"
**Creator:** Jansen (Honest Brands) | **URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RNq4-15i62U

### Workflow (Step-by-Step)
1. **Idea generation** — Use **Social One** or Facebook Ads Library to find viral competitor content
2. **Screenshot key frames** — Pause viral videos at moments that demonstrate product well; save to Notion/Word doc
3. **Create "start of scene" images** — Paste screenshot into ChatGPT → get detailed Nano Banana prompt → generate image in **Higgsfield** (Nano Banana Pro)
4. **Create "end of scene" images** — Use reference image in Nano Banana with simple prompt (e.g., "remove pen marks from wall")
5. **Generate video clips** — Upload start + end frame pairs to **Kling 3.0** with ChatGPT-written prompts
6. **Use multi-shot feature** — Kling 3.0 custom multi-shot: 2 prompts × 5s each = 10s clip (keeps B-roll engaging)
7. **Character swap** (optional) — Kling motion control: upload real footage + character image to create fake person holding product
8. **Compile all B-roll scenes** into final video

### Tools Used
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Social One** (or Kalodata/Fasmos) | Find viral TikTok Shop product videos |
| Facebook Ads Library | Competitor ad research |
| **ChatGPT** | Craft detailed prompts for Nano Banana and Kling |
| **Nano Banana** (via Higgsfield) | AI image generation — create start/end scene frames |
| **Kling 3.0** | AI video generation from start+end frame pairs |
| Notion/Word | Organize screenshots and scene descriptions |

### Inputs Needed
- Screenshots from competitor viral videos (key frames)
- Scene descriptions
- Product images
- ChatGPT for prompt engineering
- Optional: real footage of self holding product (for character swap)

### Video Structure Pattern
- **Pure B-roll compilation** — no AI avatar talking; entirely product-in-use footage
- Each scene: start frame → AI-generated motion → end frame (5-10s per scene)
- 6+ scene pairs recommended
- POV / iPhone-filmed aesthetic for realism
- Multi-shot: rapid 5s clips keep viewer engaged

### Key Insights
- **Most realistic output** — Kling 3.0 makes AI nearly undetectable
- **Start+end frame technique** is powerful for controlling AI video output
- **ChatGPT as prompt engineer** — dramatically improves Nano Banana and Kling output quality
- **Batch size increase** on Nano Banana gives variations to choose from
- **Character swap** solves the "don't want to be on camera" problem
- **This is B-roll only** — would need separate voiceover/caption solution for complete ad
- **~$20/month for Higgsfield** + Kling subscription; ~60 credits per 10-15s clip
- **Multi-shot feature** halves costs and keeps content snappy

### Platform-Specific Requirements

| Platform | Format | Length | Key Requirements |
|----------|--------|--------|-----------------|
| **TikTok Shop (organic)** | 9:16 | 15s–3min | Trending sounds (add in TikTok), CTA arrow to shop link, compliance-safe language, post same video 20x |
| **TikTok Shop (affiliate)** | 9:16 | 15s optimal | Problem→solution script, product-focused, remove brand names for flexibility |
| **Facebook/Meta Ads** | 9:16 | 30-45s | Multiple angles/scripts per product, captions essential, B-roll over avatar |
| **Shopify/Dropshipping Ads** | 9:16 | 30-45s | 3 avatars per video, strong hook, website checkout preview in CTA |
| **General e-commerce** | 9:16 | 15-20s | Ultra-realistic B-roll, POV filming style, rapid scene changes |

### Workflow Patterns (Common Across All)

```
1. RESEARCH PHASE
   ├── Find proven/selling product (TikTok Shop, Kalodata, Amazon)
   ├── Study competitors (Facebook Ad Library, Social One, TikTok)
   └── Download/screenshot winning content as reference

2. SCRIPT PHASE
   ├── Extract transcripts from viral videos
   ├── Use ChatGPT to adapt/generate scripts
   ├── Framework: Hook → Problem → Solution → Product → CTA
   └── Generate multiple angles (5+ scripts per product)

3. ASSET CREATION PHASE
   ├── AI Avatars: Arcads (premium) / CapCut Commerce Pro (free) / HeyGen / TopView
   ├── AI B-roll: Kling 3.0 / CapCut Commerce Pro Video Generator
   ├── AI Images: Nano Banana (via Higgsfield) / Krea AI
   ├── AI Voice: ElevenLabs
   └── Face Swap: aifaceswap.io / Kling character swap

4. EDITING PHASE
   ├── CapCut (universal choice across all creators)
   ├── Auto-captions with styled templates
   ├── Background music (quiet, or add in TikTok)
   └── CTA overlay with arrow/shop preview

5. DISTRIBUTION
   ├── Post multiple times (same video, 20x)
   ├── Test multiple scripts rapidly
   └── Split test avatars, hooks, angles
```

### Key Conversion Insights

1. **Script > Everything** — All creators emphasize script quality as #1 factor; tools are secondary
2. **Problem→Solution framework** dominates across all platforms
3. **Multiple angles per product** — test 5-8+ different scripts/angles; success is on 7th-8th try
4. **Volume > Perfection** — post same video 20x, create videos in 5-15 min, test rapidly
5. **3 avatars per video** outperforms single avatar (for avatar-based ads)
6. **Delayed product mention** converts better for organic TikTok (introduce at 1:15+ in 3-min video)
7. **15 seconds optimal** for TikTok Shop affiliate (paid/product-focused)
8. **Remove music before export** — add platform-native trending sounds instead
9. **B-roll overlay is mandatory** — never just show avatar talking alone
10. **Compliance matters** — avoid health claims, keep language simple and factual to avoid bans
11. **CTA with directional arrow** pointing to shop link is a specific conversion tactic
12. **Ultra-realistic B-roll** (Kling 3.0) is the cutting edge — nearly undetectable as AI

### Relevance for Craftit AI

**Opportunities identified:**
- **One-click product URL → video** is the gold standard (Creatify/TopView approach) — Craftit should match or exceed this
- **Reference video cloning** (TopView) is extremely powerful — upload viral video + product image → get similar video
- **Multi-avatar script splitting** is a manual process everywhere — could be automated
- **Start/end frame → video** workflow (Kling) produces best quality but is manual and complex — automation opportunity
- **Script generation from competitor intelligence** is done ad-hoc in ChatGPT — could be integrated
- **Face swap is a common need** but requires separate tool — could be built-in
- **Platform-specific export** (TikTok vs Meta vs Shopify) with appropriate length/format/CTA — no tool does this well yet
- **Batch testing** (generate 10 versions with different scripts/avatars) — partially exists in TopView but could be much better
