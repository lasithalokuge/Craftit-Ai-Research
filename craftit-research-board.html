Top User Pain Points
Ranked by frequency and intensity across Reddit communities, app reviews, and forums.

Pricing & Credit Systems Feel Predatory 10/10

Credits burn fast, failed outputs still consume credits. Users feel punished for experimenting.

"Charging credits during beta is unacceptable." — r/RunwayML
2

Poor Prompt Adherence 9/10

Outputs feel random. Getting exact results requires dozens of regenerations.

"RANDOM generations, terrible prompt adherence, constantly changing face..." — r/RunwayML
3

Steep Learning Curve 9/10

Even "simple" tools require prompt engineering. ComfyUI needs Python knowledge.

4

Anatomical Artifacts 8/10

Hands, fingers, limbs mangled across all tools. Bodies warp in video.

"The hands and legs are utterly insanely mangled 99% of the gens" — r/RunwayML
5

AI Video Quality Below Expectations 8/10

Warped features, inconsistent characters, unrealistic motion, low resolution.

6

Content Filters Too Aggressive 8/10

Legitimate creative projects blocked. Reasonable prompts rejected.

"At the price we're paying, why are they restricting us on BASIC creative needs?" — r/midjourney
7

Multi-Tool Fragmentation 7/10

Users combine 3-5+ tools per project. MJ → Topaz → Runway → Premiere → ElevenLabs.

8

Subscription Fatigue 7/10

$10-50/each across multiple platforms. 3-5 subscriptions for serious creators.

9

Character Inconsistency 7/10

Can't maintain same character across images or video frames. Critical for storytelling.

10

Lack of Fine Control 7/10

Can't direct camera angles, poses, lighting with precision.

Strategy & Roadmap
From research to action — phased execution plan.
🎯

Positioning

"The AI creation tool for everyone — especially those who've never created before."

The AI-first Canva. Not the simplified Runway.

NOW Quick Wins (0-4 weeks)

  • Template-first entry points — "Birthday Card," "Product Ad," "Social Post" — not "Image Generation"
  • AI-assisted prompting — simple words in, enhanced prompt behind the scenes
  • Transparent pricing — flat monthly, no credit anxiety, generous free tier
  • One-tap sharing — Done → WhatsApp / Instagram / TikTok
  • Reframe the product — "AI Creative Platform" not "AI Image Generator"

NEXT Ship the Moat (1-3 months)

  • Content Creation Canvas — AI gen + design tools combined. THE differentiator. 🔴 CRITICAL
  • Auto-mode long video — idea → script → storyboard → video. Category-defining. 🔴 CRITICAL
  • Style/brand saving — consistent output across sessions. Converts casual → paid.
  • Character persistence — save characters, maintain identity. Unlocks storytelling.
  • Reference image improvements — bridge "cool toy" to "useful for MY life"

LATER Scale (3-6 months)

  • Mobile-first app — blue ocean for SEA/India/LatAm
  • Community 2.0 — contests, remixing, galleries, growth flywheel
  • Batch/bulk generation — unlocks high-volume creators
  • Team/collaboration — shared brand kits, multi-seat
  • Kids/Education tier — safe, moderated, school partnerships

PRICING Recommended Tiers

  • Free ($0) — generous generations, no watermarks. Hook them.
  • Starter ($7-9/mo) — beginners, students. More gens, HD export.
  • Pro ($15-19/mo) — creators, SMBs. Unlimited gen, brand kit, auto-mode video.
  • Team ($35-49/mo) — multi-seat, shared brand, collaboration.
  • Undercut Runway ($76). Match Pika ($8-28). Beat Canva Pro ($13) on AI. Never charge for failures.

⏰ Timing Advantage

Ship Canvas + Auto-mode Long Video in 2026 and own the "complete AI creative suite for everyone" position before Canva catches up on AI quality or Runway simplifies their UX.
