🎯 The #1 Finding

Every successful AI video ad workflow follows the same meta-pattern: Find what already works → Clone the winning psychology → Adapt for your product → Generate many variations → Test. The tools that win are the ones that make this loop fastest with the fewest inputs.

🎬 The 13 SELL Video Types

Each type has a specific structure, audience psychology, and use case. Here's what we found about how AI can generate each:

1. UGC (Sales-Focused)

Creator-style authentic video showing real-life product usage. Feels like a real person recommending, not a brand ad.

Hook → Personal story → Product demo → Soft CTA

2. Product Promo

Polished promotional video with key benefits, stunning visuals, and selling points.

Cinematic hook → Feature showcase → Benefits → CTA

3. Advertisement

Direct, high-impact marketing with attention-grabbing hooks and strong messaging.

Pattern interrupt → Problem → Solution → Urgent CTA

4. Product Launch

High-energy introduction of a new product — excitement, exclusivity, early access.

Teaser → Reveal → Features → Limited availability CTA

5. Problem–Solution

Presents a relatable problem, introduces product as the answer, ends with strong CTA.

Pain point → Agitate → Solution → Proof → CTA

6. Feature Breakdown

Structured video explaining key features and how each solves a specific need.

Overview → Feature 1 → Feature 2 → Feature 3 → CTA

7. Offer / Discount

Sales-driven video centered around a promotion to trigger urgency.

Attention hook → Offer details → Value stack → Deadline CTA

8. Limited Time Campaign

High-urgency video emphasizing scarcity and countdowns.

Urgency hook → What's ending → Why act now → Countdown CTA

9. Retargeting Ad

Reminder-style video for people who already showed interest.

Recognition → Reminder → New angle/proof → Return CTA

10. Shopify Product Video

E-commerce optimized for product pages — benefits clearly, concisely.

Product hero → Key benefits → Social proof → Buy CTA

11. Amazon Listing Video

Marketplace-optimized — features, usage, value quickly to improve listing conversions.

Product in use → Features → Comparison → Purchase CTA

12. TikTok Shop Video

Short, fast-paced, vertical — tailored for in-app shopping and impulse buying.

Scroll-stop hook → Quick demo → Social proof → Shop now

13. Comparison / Versus

Side-by-side comparison positioning your product as the better choice.

Challenge setup → Side-by-side → Winner reveal → CTA

🏗️ Universal Video Ad Structure

The Hook → Body → CTA Framework

This is THE proven framework. Every tool, every tutorial, every winning ad follows it.

H Hook (1-3s) Stop the scroll. Pattern interrupt, bold claim, relatable pain.
B Body (10-40s) Deliver value. Problem → Solution → Proof. Show product in action.
C CTA (3-5s) Drive action. Clear, urgent, specific next step.

🔑 Key Patterns & Insights

Top 10 Insights Across All 20 Videos

  1. Script > Visuals. Multiple creators emphasize: a mediocre-looking ad with a great hook outperforms a beautiful ad with a weak script. Invest in script quality above everything.
  2. Clone, don't create. Every tactical video starts with finding proven winners and adapting them. No one creates from scratch. The best workflow: find → transcribe → adapt → generate.
  3. Volume + testing beats perfection. Generate 10-50 variations. Test script × actor combinations. Find winners. Scale with more variations of what works.
  4. B-roll is mandatory. Never show just a talking head. Interleave product demos, lifestyle shots, and motion graphics. Speed up B-roll ~20% to hide AI artifacts.
  5. Speech-to-speech is the quality differentiator. Recording your own voice with natural inflection and having AI clone that tonality is dramatically more realistic than text-to-speech.
  6. Product placement is the hardest technical challenge. Getting products to look natural in AI hands requires workarounds: product grids, lighting-matching prompts, start/end frame control.
  7. 3 avatars > 1 avatar. Splitting scripts across 3 different AI actors creates montage-style ads that feel more dynamic and perform better.
  8. Platform-native feel wins. Must look like organic UGC, not polished brand content. "Phone, front camera, selfie" in prompts is critical for authenticity.
  9. 15s for e-commerce, 30s for brand, 3min for organic. Optimal lengths vary dramatically by platform and purpose.
  10. Post same video 20x. On TikTok, repetition matters. The algorithm shows different audiences each time. Don't just post once.
⚠️ What AI Still Can't Do Well: Genuine emotional connection, trauma/health sensitivity, complex hand-product interactions, consistent character across many scenes, and platform-native trending sounds (must add in-app).
